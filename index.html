Modern presidents, along with the rest of the executive bureaucracy, have not fared well in their military adventures
In the Vietnamese War alone, nearly sixty thousand American lives were sacrificed only for the US to lose to the North Vietnamese.
An AUMF, Authorization for Use of Military Force, from two decades ago still remains in effect to this day and is used to justify conflicts that benefit a political party.
Since World War II, there has not been a single Congressional declaration of war. For a half century, presidents have abused their powers to wage war without the consent of the people.
